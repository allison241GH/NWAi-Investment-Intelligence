pipeline-monitor

company-researcher
Agent · triggered by /diligence
Web research on founders, funding history, traction signals, and public red flags. Returns an IC-ready briefing.
🗺️
competitive-intelligence
Agent · triggered by /diligence
Maps competitors & incumbents, validates market sizing, assesses moat positioning relative to the landscape.

nwai-investment-framework
Skill · 7 reference docs loaded on demand
Loads NWAi's proprietary frameworks: gates, scout questions, diligence rubrics, DD checklist, AI moats, report & memo formats.
